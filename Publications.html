LIME-LAB

The Laser Interaction with Matter at Extremes group was founded in 2016 and currently comprises eight PhD researchers. The group is primarily focused on Intense Laser Plasma Interaction, Table Top Accelerators, Nuclear Fusion with Lasers, and Proton Beam Therapy.
